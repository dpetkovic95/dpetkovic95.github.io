About Me

Hello there, my name is Dušan Petković and I am an aspiring junior programmer always looking for a good challenge. Long before I graduated with my Bachelor of Computer Science degree, I've dreamed about working as a part of a team, but little did I know that after running my first "Hello World" my friend taught me how to do it, I would be hooked. Even before, I have noticed that befriending people and solving problems is my strong suit and that is one of the reasons I choose this career path. Determination, ambitiousness, and hard work are essential for every person who aspires to do anything, but no programmer didn't have at least one anxiety attack while braining whole day about a code which just doesn't want to run, only to find out that you've missed one single semicolon. It's that anxiety exactly that taught me that patience and hard work do pay off eventually, rewarding me with pride and a smile at the end of the day. But, as you well know, life is supposed to be lived, and for such you need to work, so I believe the time has come to put all of that effort and lost nerves to the test by joining you and your team. My biggest wish is to learn as much as I can, and with your help and time, I think I would become a great addition to the team.
